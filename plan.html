Goals

The main purpose of website would be to promote the Australian music and to represents the various kinds of music styles in Townsville Academy. Moreover, our main motive is to give platform to local musicians and give them chance to perform at various theaters. Client who will explore our website, would know about the next upcoming events and can also become member of this music academy.

Success Evaluation

For the success evaluation, on the Home page we provide the latest information about the events and give the social media links to attract the audience. With the help of this, user will get to know our upcoming events and probably client might think about to participate in any particular event.
The Success of this website will be evaluated by the increasing number of members and people who registerd for various events. Moreover, if we will have more sponsors, who take interest in our events, then our music academy will be benifited more for exploring more music.

Target Audience

  • People who love the music and like to watch the music shows.
  • People who want to participate in music events.
  • peolpe who have talent but do not have any platform to show their talent.

Content

  1. Home
    • Slider of promotioanal images and videos.
    • Latest news updation and information about upcoming events.
    • Social media links for example Facebook, Instagram, Twitter.

  2. Members
    • Registration form to be a member of music academy.

  3. Events
    • Information about past events and latest events. For example Venue, Date and time of event.
    • Registration form to take entry to watch the events.

  4. Artists
    • List of Artists and links to single detailed information about artist.

  5. Bulletins
    • General news of interest.
    • Advertisement about sale or something, which needs approval from community before displaying on website.

  6. Sponsors
    • Information about sponsors who are taking interest to sponsor the events.

  7. About Us
    • Some background information and history about Townsville Music Centre.
    • Information about how to participate in events.
